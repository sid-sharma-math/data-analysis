# Data Analysis

A collection of notebooks exploring statistical analysis, and probabilistic reasoning. These are implemented from mathematical first principles.

Some projects have roots in coursework from my MSc in Computer Simulation in Science (CSIS) at the University of Wuppertal; others are independent explorations of topics I find interesting or practically useful.

## Contents

### `bayesian-reasoning/`
Notebooks on Bayesian inference and probabilistic belief updating.

| Notebook | Topic |
|----------|-------|
| `bayesian_inference_medical_testing.ipynb` | Sequential Bayesian updating — how base rates dominate test accuracy in medical diagnosis |
| `bayesian_root_cause_attribution.ipynb` | Multi-source attribution — tracing a defective outcome back to its most probable origin |
| `bayesian_job_search.ipynb` | Belief updating under uncertainty — modelling sequential outcomes in a competitive selection process |
